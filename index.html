Gurucharan Marthi Krishna Kumar

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Neuroscience at McGill University, working with Dr. Amir Shmuel and Dr. Janine Mendola.

My research focuses on AI in Neuroscience, particularly medical imaging, computer vision, and deep learning. I aim to develop automated pipelines and innovative solutions for healthcare applications.

Research

  • Generative AI: Foundation models for multimodal medical applications
  • Neuroimaging: Visual pathway, optic nerve, diffusion protocols
