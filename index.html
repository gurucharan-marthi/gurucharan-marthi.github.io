🔬 Research

My research focuses on developing automated deep learning pipelines for medical imaging, with an emphasis on segmentation 🔪, anomaly detection 🚨, and multimodal AI integration 🔗 for visual pathway analysis and optic nerve imaging 👁️.

MedVisionLlama: Leveraging Pre-Trained Large Language Model Layers to Enhance Medical Image Segmentation
Gurucharan Marthi Krishna Kumar, Aman Chadha, Janine Mendola, Amir Shmuel
Preprint, 2025

🔬 Main Field: Medical Image Segmentation 🔪
🧠 Key Result: This work integrates frozen transformer blocks from pre-trained Large Language Models (LLMs) into a medical image segmentation pipeline, significantly improving segmentation accuracy 📈 in medical imaging tasks by leveraging LLM capabilities in visual domains.
NestedMorph: Enhancing Deformable Medical Image Registration with Nested Attention Mechanisms
Gurucharan Marthi Krishna Kumar, Janine Mendola, Amir Shmuel
WACV, 2025

✨ Main Field: Medical Image Registration 📍
🚀 Key Result: NestedMorph introduces a deformable image registration framework with nested attention, enabling more effective capture of complex spatial relationships and leading to superior alignment accuracy 🎯 for intricate neuroanatomical structures.
Multimodal Medical Image Retrieval System for Clinical Decision Support System
Gurucharan Marthi Krishna Kumar, Vijay Jeyakumar, Siddarth S
Online, Nov 2024 (Undergraduate Work)

📊 Main Field: Medical Image Retrieval 🔍 & Clinical Decision Support ⚕️
💡 Key Result: This project developed a comprehensive multimodal retrieval system that effectively combines imaging data with associated metadata, demonstrating its ability to rapidly retrieve relevant cases ⚡ to support clinical diagnosis and treatment planning.
