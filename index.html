Gurucharan Marthi Krishna Kumar

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Neuroscience at McGill University, affiliated with the Montreal Neurological Institute. My work is supervised by Dr. Amir Shmuel and Dr. Janine Mendola.

My research lies at the convergence of Artificial Intelligence, Medical Imaging, and Neuroscience. I specialize in developing automated deep learning pipelines for analyzing the optic nerve and visual pathways using diffusion MRI and structural imaging. I’m especially interested in using foundation models and generative AI for building intelligent healthcare solutions.

My broader goal is to create practical, interpretable, and scalable tools that improve medical diagnostics and neuroscientific understanding using cutting-edge deep learning techniques.
