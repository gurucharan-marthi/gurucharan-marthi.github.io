Gurucharan Marthi Krishna Kumar

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Neuroscience at McGill University, working under the supervision of Dr. Amir Shmuel and Dr. Janine Mendola at the Montreal Neurological Institute.

My research lies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Medical Imaging, and Neuroscience. I develop automated deep learning-based pipelines for analyzing the visual pathway using diffusion MRI and structural imaging. I am also interested in leveraging foundation models and multimodal data for scalable medical diagnostics.

My broader goal is to use advanced computer vision and deep learning to build real-world healthcare tools that are interpretable, generalizable, and clinically meaningful.
